<Badge variant="pro">Pro</Badge>

grith Pro adds team collaboration, centralised policy management, and usage analytics on top of everything in the Community edition. It is designed for teams of up to 25 engineers who need shared security policies and visibility into AI agent usage across the organisation.

## Pricing

**$25 per user per month**, billed monthly or annually (annual billing saves 20%).

<Terminal command="grith pro upgrade" />

This opens the upgrade flow in your browser. You can also manage your subscription from the [grith.ai dashboard](https://grith.ai/dashboard).

## What Pro adds

### Team dashboard

A centralised web dashboard at [grith.ai/dashboard](https://grith.ai/dashboard) that aggregates telemetry from all team members who opt in to `grith pro sync`. View team-wide decision distributions, cost breakdowns, filter performance, and security posture — all from one place.

### Centralised policy management

Define security policies once and push them to the entire team. Team admins set filter thresholds, allowed/denied paths, and notification routing from the web dashboard. Team members receive updated policies on their next `grith pro sync`.

### Shared filter configurations

Create and share custom filter chain configurations across the team. When a team member discovers a useful filter tuning for a specific project type, they can publish it to the team library.

### Usage analytics

Aggregated usage metrics across the team: sessions per day, cost per user, provider utilisation, and tool call patterns. Identify which models deliver the best results for your team's workload.

### Email support

Priority email support from the grith team. Community users get GitHub Issues; Pro users get direct access via support@grith.ai with a 24-hour response SLA.

### Up to 25 users

Pro supports teams of up to 25 users. For larger teams or organisations that need SSO, RBAC, and compliance features, see the Enterprise plan.

## Getting started with Pro

<Tabs items={["New user", "Existing Community user"]} storageKey="pro-getting-started">
<div>

Install grith, then activate Pro:

<Terminal command="grith pro login" />

This opens your browser to authenticate on grith.ai. After authentication, your license is stored locally and grith unlocks Pro features.

</div>
<div>

If you already have grith installed and configured, upgrading is one command:

<Terminal command="grith pro upgrade" />

Your existing configuration, audit history, and filter tunings are preserved. Pro features are available immediately after authentication.

</div>
</Tabs>

<Callout type="info" title="No data leaves your machine by default">
Installing Pro does not automatically upload any data. Team sync is opt-in. Until you run `grith pro sync`, all telemetry remains local. See [Team Sync and Policy](/docs/pro/team-sync-and-policy) for details on what is shared and what is not.
</Callout>
